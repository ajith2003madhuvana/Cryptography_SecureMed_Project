Welcome to Secure Healthcare Platform

This platform ensures secure communication and data exchange between doctors and patients using advanced cryptography techniques.

How it works:

  • For Patients: Login to your account, upload your medical data, and encrypt it securely. Share the encrypted data with your doctor along with the downloaded private key.
  • For Doctors: Login to your account, receive the encrypted medical data from your patient, and decrypt it using the private key.

Ensure your private key is handled securely to maintain the confidentiality and integrity of the medical data.
