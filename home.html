Welcome, Patient!

You can securely upload and encrypt your medical data on this platform. Ensure that your sensitive information is protected and only accessible to authorized doctors.

Steps for Encrypting and Sharing Data:

  • Step 1: Click on the "Encrypt Data" option in the navigation bar to upload your medical reports.
  • Step 2: Use the encryption feature to secure your data. A private key will be generated for you to share with your doctor.
  • Step 3: Share the encrypted data and the private key with your doctor through the designated method.

This process ensures that your medical information remains confidential and is only accessible to the intended recipient. If you have any issues, please contact support.
