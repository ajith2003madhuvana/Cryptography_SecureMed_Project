This platform allows you to securely access and decrypt your patients' medical data. Please handle all information with the utmost care and confidentiality.

How to Decrypt Patient Information:

  • Step 1: Navigate to the "Decrypt Patient Info" section using the navigation bar above.

  • Step 3: Enter the decryption key shared by the patient to access the original medical records.

Ensure the decryption key remains secure and is not shared with unauthorized individuals. If you encounter any issues, please contact the technical support team.
